How to Play

Time Trial

You got 60 seconds to answer as many questions as you can

Three Strikes

You got 3 lives, answer poorly 3 times and you're out!

Categories

You got 5 lives to answer as many questions as you can in the category that you choose

Multiplayer

Penalty shootout-like gamemode. Score more questions than your opponent to win!
