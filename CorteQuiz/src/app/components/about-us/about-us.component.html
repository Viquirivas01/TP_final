How to Play

Time Trial

You have 60 seconds to answer as many questions as you can.

Three Strikes

You get 3 lives. Answer incorrectly 3 times and you're out!

Categories

You have 5 lives to answer questions in your chosen category.

Multiplayer

Penalty shootout-style mode. Score more than your opponent to win!
