How to Play

  • Time trial: You got 60 seconds to answer as many questions as you can.
  • Three Strikes: You got 3 lives, answer poorly 3 times and you're out!
  • Categories: You got 5 lives to answer as many questions as you can before running out of lives in the category that you choose
